Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and in some regions, bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel during a billing period, you'll continue to have access to the paid features until the end of that billing cycle.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits or students?

Yes! We offer special discounts for verified students, educators, and non-profit organizations. Please contact our support team with proof of status to learn more about our educational and non-profit pricing.

What happens if I exceed my monthly image limit?

If you exceed your monthly image limit on a paid plan, you'll have the option to purchase additional image packs or upgrade to a higher plan. On the free plan, you'll need to wait until your monthly quota resets or upgrade to a paid plan.

Is there a limit on how I can use the generated images?

Free plan images include a watermark and are for personal use only. Paid plans include a commercial license that allows you to use the images in commercial projects, with attribution not required. However, you cannot resell or redistribute the images as-is.
